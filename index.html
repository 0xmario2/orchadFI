📖 Getting Started

Welcome to OrchardFi — the decentralized yield protocol that automatically compounds your crypto assets across multiple chains to maximize your returns.

Getting started takes less than 2 minutes:

  1. Connect a compatible Web3 wallet (MetaMask, WalletConnect, Coinbase Wallet)
  2. Browse the available pools and select one matching your risk tolerance
  3. Deposit your assets — our smart contracts handle everything from there
  4. Watch your yield compound in real-time on the dashboard
ℹ️ Note: OrchardFi is currently deployed on Ethereum mainnet, Arbitrum, BNB Chain, and Polygon. Make sure your wallet is connected to the correct network before depositing.

Supported Assets

OrchardFi currently supports: USDC, USDT, DAI, ETH, WBTC, stETH, ARB, MATIC, and BNB.

⚙️ How It Works

OrchardFi uses a layered architecture to optimize yield across DeFi protocols:

Deposit Flow

When you deposit, your assets flow through the following pipeline:

User Deposit → Vault Contract → Strategy Router → Protocol Adapters → Underlying Protocols (USDC) (ERC-4626) (Risk Engine) (Uniswap, Curve) (Liquidity Pools)

Compounding

The Keeper Network calls our harvest functions every 4–12 hours, converting earned rewards back into the base asset and reinvesting — giving you compound interest automatically.

The effective APY from compounding follows: APY = (1 + APR/n)^n - 1 where n = harvests per year.

Withdrawal Flow

Withdrawals are instant for pools with available liquidity. In rare cases of deep withdrawal, a queuing mechanism ensures fair ordering with no slippage guarantees broken.

⚠️ Risk: While OrchardFi takes every precaution, DeFi protocols carry inherent smart contract risk. Never deposit more than you can afford to lose.

🔬 Yield Engine

The OrchardFi Yield Engine is the core of the protocol — a modular, upgradeable system that sources and optimizes yield across the DeFi ecosystem.

Strategy Selection

Each vault runs one or more strategies. The Strategy Router selects the optimal allocation based on:

  • Current APY across candidate protocols
  • Gas costs amortized over the deposit size
  • Protocol risk scores from our security oracle
  • Liquidity depth and withdrawal speed

Rebalancing

Automated rebalancing occurs when yield deviation exceeds 5% relative to the optimal allocation, or when a protocol's risk score drops below our threshold.

Fee Structure

  • Performance Fee: 10% of yield earned (not principal)
  • Withdrawal Fee: 0.1% on withdrawal amount
  • No deposit fee

Fees are directed to the DAO treasury and ORCH stakers.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How often does compounding happen? ▼

Our Keeper Network harvests and compounds rewards every 4–12 hours depending on gas costs and reward accumulation. High-yield pools compound more frequently.

Are there any lock-up periods? ▼

No. All pools on OrchardFi are flexible — you can withdraw your full balance at any time. There is a 0.1% withdrawal fee to deter flash-loan manipulation.

What is impermanent loss? ▼

Impermanent loss (IL) occurs in liquidity pools when the price ratio of paired assets changes after deposit. Our stablecoin pools have near-zero IL. Our LP pools display estimated IL risk in the pool details. ORCH rewards are partially designed to offset IL.

Is OrchardFi audited? ▼

Yes. We've completed two independent audits with CertiK (score: 97/100) and PeckShield (0 critical findings). Audit reports are publicly available on our Audit page.

How are APYs calculated? ▼

APYs are calculated based on the last 7 days of reward data, annualized and accounting for compounding frequency. They are estimates and will fluctuate with market conditions.

How do I earn ORCH tokens? ▼

ORCH tokens are distributed to depositors proportionally based on their share of total TVL in each pool. The ORCH emission rate is governed by the DAO and adjusts monthly.
